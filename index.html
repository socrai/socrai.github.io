Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the program?

The program runs for 4 weeks during the summer and has 2 cohorts, one starting in June and another starting in July!

Are there any prerequisites for applying?

No, there is no required experience to participate in this program!

How and what do students learn?

The first week of the program is dedicated to learning the fundamentals of machine learning, and the next 3 weeks are dedicated to honing skills through a comprehensive research program with mentor guidance!
